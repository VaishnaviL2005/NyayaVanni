# Code of Conduct

## ↪️ Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## ↪️ Community Standards

### Positive Behaviour

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Showing empathy toward other community members
- Supporting and encouraging fellow contributors

### Unacceptable Behaviour

Examples of unacceptable behaviour include:

- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Personal attacks or public/private harassment
- Sharing someone’s private information without permission
- Any conduct that creates an unsafe or unwelcoming environment

---

## ↪️ Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing community standards and may remove or reject contributions, comments, or interactions that violate this Code of Conduct.

---

## ↪️ Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and community interactions.

---

## ↪️ Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the project maintainers for review and appropriate action.

All reports will be reviewed respectfully and fairly.

---

## ↪️ Enforcement

Contributors who violate the Code of Conduct may face warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent removal from the community depending on the severity of the violation.

---
